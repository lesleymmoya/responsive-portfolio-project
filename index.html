About Me

I am passionate about design and tech.

I have a BFA in Fine Arts and Textile/Surface Design from the Fashion Institute of Technology in NYC where I worked as a textile/CAD artist before relocating to Taos, NM in 2011.
I started my textile studio hunt+gather in 2011 which sells original designs to the home furnishings and fashion markets and have been freelancing ever since. In 2017 I started a clothing line called Roxie Rudolph which is fun, comfortable clothing featuring bold prints.
After building a few websites for my businesses over the years, I started to get really interested in learning how to code and build websites from scratch. I am currently enrolled in Skillcrush's Break into Tech Blueprint so that I can build on the design skills that I already possess.
